# Lot Size

## What is Lot size?

**Lot size** determines the **volume** of your trade. It represents how much currency you're buying or selling.

- 1 Standard Lot = 100,000 units
- Controls your **profit** and **loss** per pip

**لاٹ سائز کیا ہے؟**  
لاٹ سائز آپ کی ٹریڈ کی مقدار کا تعین کرتا ہے۔

---

## How many types of lot sizes?

There are **four main types**:

### 1. **Standard Lot**
- Volume: **100,000 units**
- 1 Pip ≈ $10 (for USD pairs)

### 2. **Mini Lot**
- Volume: **10,000 units**
- 1 Pip ≈ $1 (for USD pairs)

### 3. **Micro Lot**
- Volume: **1,000 units**
- 1 Pip ≈ $0.10 (for USD pairs)

### 4. **Nano Lot**
- Volume: **100 units**
- 1 Pip ≈ $0.01 (for USD pairs)

**لاٹ سائز کی کتنی اقسام ہیں؟**  
چار اقسام: اسٹینڈرڈ، منی، مائیکرو، اور نینو لاٹ۔

---

## What is the lot sizing Schedule on account balance?

### Recommended Lot Size by Account Balance:

| Account Balance | Recommended Lot Size | Risk Per Trade |
|----------------|---------------------|----------------|
| $100 - $500 | **0.01 (Micro)** | Max 2% |
| $500 - $2,000 | **0.05 - 0.10** | Max 2% |
| $2,000 - $5,000 | **0.10 - 0.25** | Max 2% |
| $5,000 - $10,000 | **0.25 - 0.50** | Max 2% |
| $10,000+ | **0.50 - 1.00** | Max 2% |

**Golden Rule:** Never risk more than **1-2%** of your account per trade.

**اکاؤنٹ بیلنس کے مطابق لاٹ سائز**  
چھوٹے اکاؤنٹ = چھوٹا لاٹ، بڑا اکاؤنٹ = بڑا لاٹ۔ کبھی بھی 2٪ سے زیادہ رسک نہ لیں۔

---

## What is an Order?

An **Order** is an instruction to your broker to execute a trade at a specific price or condition.

Types:
- Buy/Sell immediately
- Buy/Sell at a future price
- Close a trade automatically

**آرڈر کیا ہے؟**  
آرڈر آپ کے بروکر کو ٹریڈ کرنے کی ہدایت ہے۔

---

# Market Orders

## How many types of Orders?

There are **three main types**:

1. **Market Order** (Instant Execution)
2. **Limit Order** (Pending Order)
3. **Stop Order** (Pending Order)

**آرڈرز کی کتنی اقسام ہیں؟**  
تین اہم اقسام: مارکیٹ، لمٹ، اور اسٹاپ آرڈر۔

---

## What is Market Order?

A **Market Order** executes **immediately** at the **current market price**.

### Example:
- EUR/USD is trading at **1.1000**
- You place a **Buy Market Order**
- Trade opens **instantly** at 1.1000 (or very close)

**Best for:**
- Quick entries
- News trading
- Breakout trading

**مارکیٹ آرڈر کیا ہے؟**  
مارکیٹ آرڈر فوری طور پر موجودہ قیمت پر عمل میں آتا ہے۔

---

## What is Limit Order?

A **Limit Order** is a **pending order** that executes when price reaches a **better price** than current.

### Buy Limit:
- Set **below** current price
- Example: EUR/USD = 1.1000, Buy Limit at 1.0950
- Trade opens if price **drops** to 1.0950

### Sell Limit:
- Set **above** current price
- Example: EUR/USD = 1.1000, Sell Limit at 1.1050
- Trade opens if price **rises** to 1.1050

**Best for:**
- Buying at support
- Selling at resistance

**لمٹ آرڈر کیا ہے؟**  
لمٹ آرڈر ایک پینڈنگ آرڈر ہے جو بہتر قیمت پر کھلتا ہے۔

---

## What is Stop Order?

A **Stop Order** is a **pending order** that executes when price reaches a **worse price** than current (breakout trading).

### Buy Stop:
- Set **above** current price
- Example: EUR/USD = 1.1000, Buy Stop at 1.1050
- Trade opens if price **rises** to 1.1050 (breakout)

### Sell Stop:
- Set **below** current price
- Example: EUR/USD = 1.1000, Sell Stop at 1.0950
- Trade opens if price **drops** to 1.0950 (breakdown)

**Best for:**
- Breakout trading
- Momentum trading

**اسٹاپ آرڈر کیا ہے؟**  
اسٹاپ آرڈر بریک آؤٹ ٹریڈنگ کے لیے استعمال ہوتا ہے۔
